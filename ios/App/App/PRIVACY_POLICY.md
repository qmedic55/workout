# VitalPath Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 18, 2024**

## Introduction

VitalPath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the VitalPath mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you sign in with Apple, we collect:
- Email address (if provided)
- Name (first and last)
- Apple ID user identifier

### Health and Fitness Data
With your permission, we collect:
- Body measurements (weight, waist, hips, chest)
- Nutrition data (calories, macronutrients, food entries)
- Activity data (steps, workouts, exercise type)
- Sleep data (hours, quality ratings)
- Biometric data (heart rate, HRV, if synced from devices)
- Subjective assessments (energy levels, mood, stress)

### Usage Data
We may collect:
- App usage patterns
- Feature interactions
- Error logs and diagnostics
- Device information (model, iOS version)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Personalized Coaching**: Generate AI-powered health recommendations
- **Track Your Progress**: Display trends, charts, and insights
- **Improve Our Service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **Communicate**: Send important updates about your account
- **Ensure Security**: Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers using industry-standard encryption
- We use HTTPS for all data transmission
- Your health data is stored in compliance with applicable regulations
- We implement access controls and regular security audits

## Apple Health Integration

With your explicit permission:
- We read health data from Apple Health (steps, heart rate, sleep, etc.)
- We write workout data back to Apple Health
- You control which data types we can access
- You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings

## AI and Machine Learning

- We use AI to provide personalized coaching recommendations
- Your data is used to generate insights specific to you
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- AI processing happens on our secure servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update incorrect information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export**: Download your data in JSON or CSV format
- **Opt-out**: Stop using health data features

## Data Retention

- We retain your data while your account is active
- You can delete your account at any time
- Upon deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days
- Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Apple Sign In**: For authentication
- **Backend API**: Hosted on Replit (health-mentor-ai--ikugelman.replit.app)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

VitalPath is designed for adults 40+ and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

## Legal Compliance

VitalPath complies with:
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) principles

## Your Consent

By using VitalPath, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
